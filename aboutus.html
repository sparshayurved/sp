How ayurvedic doctor help you online !

Help

Online consultations have become the norm of the day because of the ongoing pandemic situation. Perhaps their bittersweet feature is the remoteness of patients from their physician. Here the critics' right to question - how can you get the assurance of healing without the sensation of touch? But what’s interesting is that the solution is readily provided by Ayurveda itself.
As per Ayurvedic science, 8 examination features of a person, namely Nadi (Pulse), Mala (Stool quality), Mootra (Urine texture), Jihwa (Tongue), Shabda (Sound), Sparsha (Touch), Drik (Eye) and Akriti (Physical built) should be studied to sketch his/her Prakriti or medical profile. Added to this information is the family history and verbal feedback provided by the patient. Evidently, it is a detailed assessment that doesn’t fall short of insights about a person’s health. It is by the same token that online Ayurvedic consultations show a more promising rate of success.
Ayurveda practitioners may speak with you over call or video chat to understand the nature of your complaint and analyse your physical features like teeth, nails, face, hair, tongue etc. Here, all 7 examination features, except the body pulse can be noted to make a personalized diagnosis.

Get rid of Chronic diseases!

Diseases

Ayurveda is an ancient Indian medical science, which specialises in treating chronic diseases. It also aims to maintain a healthy body, mind and spirit. A healthy person should follow the right dincharya (daily routine), adapt to seasonal changes and eat a nutritious diet to avoid any disease.In its own terms, expertly practiced Ayurveda can definitely yield reliable, efficacious results when applied to all manner of chronic diseases.Chronic diseases are long-lasting conditions that usually can be controlled but not cured. People living with chronic illnesses often must manage daily symptoms that affect their quality of life, and experience acute health problems and complications that can shorten their life expectancy.The science of Ayurveda is based on the very compounds that make and run your body. It tries to treat the underlying causes of the problem rather than an organ or part of the body that is affected. This way Ayurveda can be highly effective in healing mild as well as chronic diseases, binging back the body equilibrium.
